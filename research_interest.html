Research Interest

1. Optimization

Optimization Theory — boundary estimation for model performance and the training process. Possible research tools include KKT conditions[6].

Fine-tuning — focusing on the Hessian matrix spectrum and randomly masked parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT)[1][2].

2. High Performance Computing

MLSys — studying scheduling for training and inference of large models, for instance, task-graph partitioning and pipeline parallelism on multi-GPU and heterogeneous resources to improve end-to-end throughput and latency consistency[3].

Federated and edge learning — for example, the limitations of differential privacy and better privacy-preserving methods such as homomorphic encryption[5].

Reducing model size — research directions include pruning and sparsification, data storage methods, algorithm limitations, and improvements[4].

3. AI Healthcare

Nervous system, Physiology and Diseases - I am working on the physiological basis, course of disease, and personalized prediction and treatment of diseases such as Alzheimer's disease. In the future may work on depression, anxiety, and vestibular migraine.

Medical Image Processing - May focus on MRI figure segementation, Multimodal image fusion, 3D reconstruction

AI Healthcare Agent - Developing knowledge-enhanced, interpretable large models for personalized medicine and auxiliary diagnosis

4. Applied Mathematics

Operation Research - Quantative finance, Machine learning
